1st NIDC Intra Nis Debate Competition- 2019

The first competition organized by Banasree National Ideal School Debating Club is "1st NIDC Intra NIS Debate Competition - 2019". The Inter-National Ideal School (Banasree Branch) Debate Competition was organized to further expand the success story of National Ideal School and to give newcomers the experience of something new. The competition was completed in tab format with 6 teams and each team got a chance to participate 3 rounds of debate.

BNISDC Clash of Orators (Fundraiser)

After the “1st NIDC Intra NIS Debate Competition - 2019”, the Banasree National Ideal School Debating Club appeared with the “BNISDC Clash of Orators”, which was an online debate competition.
The world was at a standstill because of the corona virus. Everyone was staying at home for fear of contracting the corona virus. While at home, we came up with the "BNISDC Clash of Orators" through the Discord App for Debate Lovers.
The money from the registration fee for this tournament was given to help the poor people who became helpless due to the corona virus infection. We were able to help the helpless people through the participation of 32 teams and 96 debaters from different parts of Bangladesh.
The tournament was held with 24 teams. Registration was done online. The debaters had a chance to debate 4 rounds.

For more details you can visit our facebook event of BNISDC Clash of Orators
