About Us

The debate club run by the National Ideal School is known as the Banasree National Ideal School Debating Club.
Debate has become an elite art in the twentieth century, following the path of the glorious history of debate. The triumph of youth in the power of reason is all around today. Banasree National Ideal School Debating Club-BNIDSC is committed to inculcate a sense of humanity in the students of National Ideal School by spreading this debate practice among them. On 11th October 2018, Banasree National Ideal School Debating Club was started following the initiative of some enterprising boys of Banasree Bhaban 1-A of National Ideal School. In addition to textbook based education, BNIDSC is working to make students think outside the textbook and encourage them in co-educational activities.

Workshop for You on AP and BP Debate


Asian Parliamentary Debate

Asian Parliamentary Debate is one of the more popular forms, based on debates in the Asian Parliament. It is now popular in debating competitions in Asia region. AP debates consist of two teams who debate over an issue, more commonly called a topic or proposition.




British Parliamentary Debate

British Parliamentary Style Debate is one of the more popular forms, based on debates in the British Parliament. It was first adopted by UK universities, but is now popular in debating competitions around the world.
